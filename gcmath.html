Linear behavior




m(x + d) = mx + md




  • Awesome! Can model anything, at least locally.
  • Constant slope m
  • Unconstrained y-values

Exponential behavior




b(x + d) = bxbd




  • Made for modeling chage in slope
  • Slope proportional to value
  • Y-values can be creatively limited to ranges
