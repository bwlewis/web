Making Predictions with Lines: Woolly Adelgid Data

The hemlock woolly adelgid is an insect native to East Asia. It feeds by sucking sap from hemlock and spruce trees. In its native range, HWA is not a serious pest because populations are managed by natural predators and host resistance. In eastern North America it is a destructive pest that seriously threatens hemlock trees.

Exercise:

  1. Pick 2 points on the graph where the year is less than 2023.
  2. Figure out the equation of the line that runs through those points. Write this equation down.
  3. Use that equation to predict the number of infested counties in 2023.
  4. How close was your prediction to the actual value in 2023?
  5. Does the infestation seem to be speeding up or slowing down recently?
